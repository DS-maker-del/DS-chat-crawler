ADAMS, ESQ Founder . With an extensive background in the community association industry, Adrian Adams is one of the leading attorneys in California specializing in common interest developments. Adrian founded ADAMS | STIRLING PLC and quickly grew the firm into a preeminent boutique firm specializing in highrise condominiums, stock cooperatives, business parks, lake associations, golfing communities, equestrian associations, and ocean-front communities. Davis-Stirling.com . Seeing the need to educate board members and homeowners, Adrian created a free research website called Davis-Stirling.com that quickly became the number one resource for everything related to the laws affecting residential and mixed-use common interest developments in California. He designed the website, did all the programming, wrote all the articles, and set up all the internal linking to make it easy for users to maneuver through the website.&nbsp; Adrian also publishes the ADAMS|STIRLING Newsletter that become the largest circulating newsletter of its kind in the state. Expert Witness . Adrian also serves as an expert witness on standards of care issues involving association boards and managers and has testified extensively on issues involving maintenance, mold, rules enforcement, budgets, reserves, governing documents, management, and operations.
